Group Leader

Leads a team of volunteers at a worksite

When you arrive on event day

Your first action

Open Tessera → tap your worksite card on the Dashboard → tap Check In. This confirms your group is on-site and activates your project. Your volunteers will gather around you — walk them through what you see on the project page before anyone picks up a tool.

What this role does

Leads a group of 10–15 volunteers at a homeowner's worksite
Coordinates work with the Site Coach assigned to your site
Requests materials and tools when the project needs them
Logs project progress and marks tasks complete as work is finished
Takes before and after photos of completed work
Keeps your team informed and motivated throughout the day

Pages you'll use in the app

Dashboard My Worksite Project Materials Photos Map Notifications

Basic workflow

1

Check in at your worksite

Open the app, find your worksite on the Dashboard, and tap Check In. This lets event leadership know your team is on-site and ready.

2

Review the project with your team

Open the Project page and read through the scope of work with your volunteers. Introduce yourselves to the homeowner. Walk the site together before starting.

3

Take before photos

Before any work begins, take before photos of the areas your team will be working on. This is important for the homeowner's record and the event closeout report.

4

Work the project and request materials as needed

Lead your team through the tasks. When you need supplies, tap "Request Materials" on the project page. A Material Runner will see it and deliver to your site.

5

Mark tasks complete

As each task is finished, mark it complete in the Project page. Your Site Coach can see this progress and will come check on you when tasks are done.

6

Take after photos and close out

When work is complete, take after photos of everything your team did. Then have the homeowner review the work. Your Site Coach will complete the final sign-off.

Common tasks — how to

Request materials: Go to your project page → tap "Request Materials" → describe what you need and the quantity → submit. A runner will be notified.
Upload a photo: Go to the Photos tab on your worksite → tap the camera icon → take or choose a photo → label it Before or After.
Mark a task complete: On the Project page → find the task → tap the checkbox or "Mark Complete" button.
Contact your Site Coach: Tap Notifications or find them on the Map page. You can also send them a message through the app.
Find the worksite address: Tap the Map tab — your site is pinned. Tap the pin for the address and directions.
