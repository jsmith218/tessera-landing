Role guides

Select your role

Each guide covers what your role does, which pages you'll use in the app, and exactly what to do when you arrive on event day. If you're not sure what your role is, ask your group leader or an event coordinator.

→

Group Leader

Leads a team of volunteers at a worksite. Coordinates with the Site Coach and requests materials as the project progresses.
→

Site Coach

Oversees project quality and scope. Supports group leaders, logs project progress, and connects sites to event leadership.
→

Material Runner

Delivers supplies and tools to worksites. Fulfills material requests, coordinates with the warehouse, and logs deliveries.
→

Event Coordinator

Oversees the entire event. Monitors all sites, manages logistics, handles escalations, and keeps everything moving.
→

Hotshot

A skilled float team member dispatched to worksites that need extra help. Handles specialized tasks or urgent project needs.
→

Support

Handles backend logistics — shopping, warehouse, check-in, food, and other coordination that keeps the event running.

Not sure which role you have? Check the FAQ or ask your group leader or event coordinator.
